The Race

One Mile.
No Excuses.

The Heatcheck Mile is Austin's grassroots road mile — a heat-based, all-levels race built for anyone who wants to test themselves over the purest distance in running.

Year 1 brought out everyone from sub-5 racers to first-timers. The energy was electric. The competition was real. Year 2 is going to be bigger.

Whether you're chasing a PR, racing your friends, or just want to see what you've got — this is your race.

The Course

Onion Creek, South Austin

A fast, flat out-and-back through one of South Austin's best neighborhoods. Shaded stretches, smooth roads, and a finish line you can hear from 400 meters out.

▴

Flat & Fast

Minimal elevation change. This course is built for speed.

◯

Certified Distance

Accurately measured mile. Your time counts.

★

Crowd Support

Spectators line the finish. You'll hear them coming in.

Parking available at Onion Creek amenity center. Course map will be sent to all registered runners one week before race day.
