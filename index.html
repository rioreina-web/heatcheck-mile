The Race

The Fastest
Morning in Austin.

The Heatcheck Mile is Austin's grassroots road mile — a heat-based, all-levels race built for anyone who wants to test themselves over the purest distance in running.

Year 1 brought out everyone from sub-5 racers to first-timers. The energy was electric. The competition was real. Year 2 is going to be bigger.

Whether you're chasing a PR, racing your friends, or just want to see what you've got — this is your race.

Race Format

How Heats Work

Submit your seed time when you register. At bib pickup, you'll be assigned to a heat based on your seed so you're racing people your speed. Heats build from slowest to fastest — the energy gets bigger with every wave.

The Course

Onion Creek, South Austin

A fast, flat 3-turn loop through one of South Austin's best neighborhoods. Shaded stretches, smooth roads, and a finish line you can hear from 400 meters out.
